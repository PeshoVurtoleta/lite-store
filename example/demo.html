Refetch (1.1 · reconcile)
A refetch hands you a whole new data tree. reconcile(s, next) diffs it in and patches only the changed leaves — here 3 cells re-run 3 effects and the signal pool stays flat. The footgun s.cells = fresh disposes every cell signal and re-fires all 600. Watch effects last mutation and the alloc/dispose deltas in last action.
Each cell's background-color is bound to one path: s.cells[r][c].hue. Mutating one path re-runs one effect. Mutating ten re-runs ten. The "effects last mutation" stat is the fine-grained reactivity story made visible.
Before Bind grid: zero signals allocated. The store holds 600 cells but no reactive observers exist — lazy allocation is the headline performance property.
